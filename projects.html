Projects & Research

Selected projects with short descriptions and core tech.

Autonomous Skid‑Steer Robot for Under‑Canopy Agriculture (2025–Present)

Designed and fabricated a skid‑steer robotic platform for row‑crop environments. Modeled vehicle kinematics in MATLAB and integrated ROS2‑based navigation. Developed perception and controls pipelines with multi‑camera/IMU sensing.

Keywords: ROS2, MATLAB, IMU, computer vision, navigation

Prosthetic Knee via Hybrid Manufacturing (2023–2024)

Evaluated manufacturing modes and materials, performed ISO 10328 loading simulations, and conducted static/fatigue testing of prototypes.

Keywords: SolidWorks, ANSYS, ISO 10328, testing

FSR Embedded Insole for Gait Analysis (2021–2023)

Designed and built an insole with FSR sensors for gait phase detection. Implemented ESP‑32 firmware and ML classifiers (Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, SVM). Presented at KU University Scholar Conference.

Keywords: ESP‑32, machine learning, biomechanics
